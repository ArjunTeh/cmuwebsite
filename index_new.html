Hey! My name is Arjun Teh. I am a Final Year PhD student at Carnegie Mellon University. I am co-advised by Matt O'Toole and Ioannis Gkioulekas. Previously, I attended University of Texas at Austin. My current research is inverse problems in lens design that involve geometric ray theory. My goal is to build differentiable simulators that can be used to tackle lens design and reconstruction problems.
